UTV

About

A utility terrain vehicle (UTV) or recreational off-highway vehicle (ROV) are intended to transport persons and cargo, have a non-straddle seat, are designed to travel on four or more wheels, use a steering wheel and pedals for controls.

Specification

  • Engine - 200cc
  • Drive - 2x4
  • Seating Cap. - 2
  • Fuel - Petrol
  • Gearing - CVT & Manual
  • Steering - Rack & Pinion
  • Suspension - Double wishbone
