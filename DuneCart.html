Dune kart

About

It is usually a roofless vehicle with a rear-mounted engine. A dune buggy can be created by modifying an existing vehicle or custom-building a new vehicle. And can also be Powered with electric motor also.

Specification

  • Engine - 350cc to 1100cc
  • Drive - 4x4,2x4
  • Seating Cap. - 2 to 6
  • Fuel - Petrol, CNG, Electric & diesel
  • Gearing - CVT & Manual
  • Steering - Rack & Pinion
  • Suspension - Double wishbone
